Habbo Hotel Happenings - January 2, 2002

Welcome to our regular, not-to-be-missed round up of the latest happenings in Habbo Hotel.

GET ON DOWN

Been strutting your funky stuff in our new public room? I have. Even had a bit of a New Year's Eve boogie with a few friends too!

If you have had your head stuck in virtual sand, let me fill you in. The fab new room (or two rooms, in fact) is called The Chromide Club and it's staffed by dance-obsessed Will. He loves - and lives - to strut his funky stuff, so he's very happy in his job!

And if you're a bit of a budding DJ you can spin the decks in the second room (the other room's more laid back). See if you can get those Habbos really moving...

I WILL NOT FLOOD. I WILL NOT FLOOD. I WILL NOT FLOOD.

You seen those annoying peeps who pitch up in a room and shout the same thing over and over again? Or worse still, are you one of these tiresome Habbos? If so, stop it right now! You'll only get kicked out and it doesn't make people want to visit your room. After all, who wants to be friends with an annoying person?

If you want to advertise your cool room, why don't you use a Stickie? You can put Stickies up in any private room - you don't need rights to do it.

One last thing on this subject - if you see a scammer flooding a room Call for help immediately. We must banish these meanies!

You can tell they're scammers because they say stuff about getting free Credits. And you all know that the only places you can get free Habbo Credits are the Splash-O-Matic kiosks and the Wacky Wednesday competitions. Got it? Good.

WACKY WEDNESDAY WINNERS

Two winners to announce this week - congrats to matthardy24.7 and TheCodFather. 100 free Habbo Creds are winging their way to your Purses right now!


That's all from me this week - if you've got any ideas about what you'd like to see in the hotel, or any comments on this newsletter, please email me.

Happy Habboing and Merry Christmas!

The Manager
