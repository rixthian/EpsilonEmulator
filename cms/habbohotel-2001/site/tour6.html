Habbo Console

Your Habbo Console lets you keep in constant contact with your friends, using instant messages, email and SMS, even when they're offline.

Only Habbos that you have chosen to add to your Friends List can talk to you through the Console, so there's no chance of being 'stalked' or pestered by people you don't like. The messages you send are totally private too.

The Console let's you know when someone is trying to reach you with a flashing '!' and a sound.

Click on the 'Find' button (1.) to see if one of your Habbo Friends is in the hotel.
