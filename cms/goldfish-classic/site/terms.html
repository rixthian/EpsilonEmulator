 HOTEL GOLDFISH RULES
 IN A NUTSHELL
Hotel Goldfish is a virtual hotel where young people can meet new and old friends in a safe and friendly environment.

As a user of Hotel Goldfish, you have to be considerate towards other people and behave in a way that doesn't make other users feel threatened or uncomfortable. The rules are very simple:

Read on for the complete rules of Hotel Goldfish...

Don't do this:

- Don't curse or use offensive names targetted at yourself or others.
- Don't commit racist behavior.
- Do't share yours or anyone else's personal information with strangers.
- Don't harass those who, for one reason or another, don't want to talk to you.
- Don't engage in sexual intercourse with other users, even in private rooms.
- Do not violate the hotel policies or encourage others to violate them.
Do this:

- Meet your friends and have fun.
- Meet new people.
- Respect other peoples views.
And that's all you need to start adventuring in Hotel Goldfish! If you would like to know more about Goldfish's safety, you can read our security guidelines.

Hotel Goldfish reserves the right to use any means necessary to maintain order in Goldfish. The hotel management also retains the right to revoke access to the hotel to any user, and the right to report any illegalities to the authorities (yes, we mean the police).

Read more about the rules for using Goldfish here. If you are under 14, ask your parents to check them out as well.
